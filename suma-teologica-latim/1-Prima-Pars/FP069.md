# Summa Theologica

Deinde considerandum est de opere tertiae diei. Et circa hoc quaeruntur duo.
        
We next consider the work of the third day. Under this head there are two points of inquiry:




Primo, de congregatione aquarum.
        


(1)
 About the gathering together of the waters;
      


Secundo, de productione plantarum.
        


(2)
 About the production of plants.
