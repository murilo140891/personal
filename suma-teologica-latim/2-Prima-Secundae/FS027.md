# Summa Theologica

Ad tertium dicendum quod etiam amor naturalis, qui est in omnibus rebus, causatur ex aliqua cognitione, non quidem in ipsis rebus naturalibus existente, sed in eo qui naturam instituit, ut supra dictum est.
        


Reply to Objection 3:
 Even natural love, which is in all things, is caused by a kind of knowledge, not indeed existing in natural things themselves, but in Him Who created their nature, as stated above (
Question [26]
, 
Article [1]
; cf. 
FP
, Question [6], Article [1], ad 2).
